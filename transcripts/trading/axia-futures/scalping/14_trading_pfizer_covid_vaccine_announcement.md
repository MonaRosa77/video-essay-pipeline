# Trading the Pfizer Covid Vaccine Announcement

**Source:** https://axiafutures.com/blog/trading-the-pfizer-covid-vaccine-announcement/
**Instruments:** E-mini S&P 500, E-mini Dow, DAX, Russell 2000, NASDAQ 100, Euro Stoxx, German Bunds, US 30-Year Treasuries
**Category:** Elite Trader Execution
**Trader:** Demetris Mavrommatis (Co-Founder, Head of Trading)

---

## Context

Macro event trade on the Pfizer Covid vaccine announcement. One of the largest single-day executions documented by Axia Futures.

---

## Execution Timeline

### Initial Position Building (Announcement at 11:45 AM)

- **Bought 400** E-mini S&P 500 contracts
- **Bought 90** DAX contracts
- Initial P&L: **$300,000 within seconds**

### Secondary Entry (15 seconds after announcement)

- **Added 150** E-mini Dow contracts
- **Sold 540** Bund contracts

### Total Long Exposure

**~650 lots across three equity indexes** plus 540 short Bund

---

## Trade Management

The trader scaled out equity positions after observing:

> "The Spoo had done over 50 handles in around 2 mins. DAX did over 250 ticks!"

He maintained bond shorts, anticipating delayed reaction in fixed income markets.

---

## Result

**Over $1,300,000 profit** on the day -- described as his **"best day ever."**

---

## Key Methodology

1. **Visualization:** Emphasized "visualising the huge reward" before and during execution
2. **Speed:** Full position built within 15 seconds of the news hitting wires
3. **Multi-market simultaneous execution:** 4 instruments at once (S&P, DAX, Dow, Bunds)
4. **Correlation management:** Equities long + bonds short = pure risk-on positioning
5. **Staged exits:** Scaled out equities first (quick movers), held bond shorts (slower to react)
6. **Focus through volatility:** Maintained composure during extreme price movement
