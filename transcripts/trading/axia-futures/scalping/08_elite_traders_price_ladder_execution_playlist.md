## Key Principles Across All Videos

1. **Multi-market execution:** Elite traders don't trade one instrument -- they simultaneously position across 3-4 correlated markets
2. **Speed of deployment:** Positions established within seconds of news hitting wires
3. **Correlation conviction:** When one correlated market stalls, another gives conviction to hold
4. **Scaling strategy under adversity:** How to manage when markets don't immediately cooperate
5. **Cross-asset priority ordering:** In risk-on/off environments, there's an optimal sequence for which products to hit first
