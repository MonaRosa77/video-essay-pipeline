## Central Claim / 核心论点

**English:** Meditation (打坐) is the deliberate lowering of precision weights on high-level prior beliefs in the brain's predictive coding hierarchy, and this mechanism is structurally isomorphic to the consciousness model described by Yogacara Buddhism (唯识宗) 1,500 years ago.

**中文：** 打坐的本质是主动降低大脑预测编码层级中高层先验信念的精度权重，而这一机制与1500年前唯识宗所描述的意识运作模型在数学结构上同构。

---

## Supporting Arguments / 论证结构

### 1. 大脑是预测发生器，非被动接收器
The brain is not a passive input-processing-output system. Anatomical evidence shows feedback connections (top-down) vastly outnumber feedforward connections (bottom-up). The brain generates predictions and only transmits prediction errors upward — the "controlled hallucination" model (Anil Seth).

### 2. 先验信念与精度权重决定感知
Prior beliefs (Priors) carry precision weights that determine how strongly they control perception. High-weight priors override sensory data (e.g., the blue/black vs. white/gold dress illusion). This is the mechanism behind cognitive rigidity and bias.

### 3. 预测编码三层结构 ≅ 唯识三自性
Wang maps predictive coding onto the Three Natures (三自性) of Yogacara:
- **依他起性** (Paratantra) = bottom-up sensory data stream (raw, conditionless signals)
- **遍计所执性** (Parikalpita) = top-down prior beliefs and predictions (the "stories" the brain overlays)
- **圆成实性** (Pariniṣpanna) = metacognition — seeing the prediction process itself

### 4. 自由能原理 ≅ 趋乐避苦
Friston's Free Energy Principle (minimize surprise/prediction error) maps to the Buddhist observation that all beings are driven by pursuit of pleasure and avoidance of suffering. Both describe the same fundamental drive in different formal languages.

### 5. 贪嗔痴 = 对预测误差的三种不当反应
- **贪 (Greed):** Clinging to low prediction-error states
- **嗔 (Aversion):** Rejecting high prediction-error states
- **痴 (Ignorance):** Not knowing the mechanism is operating at all

### 6. 止观 = 精度权重调节
- **止 (Śamatha/Calm Abiding):** Lowering precision weights on high-level priors, quieting the narrative machine
- **观 (Vipaśyanā/Insight):** Observing the raw data stream (依他起) without adding labels or judgments
- **止观合一:** = 圆成实 — seeing the entire prediction process without being hijacked by it

### 7. REBUS模型的神经证据
Robin Carhart-Harris & Friston's REBUS model (Relaxed Beliefs Under Psychedelics) shows that both psychedelics and deep meditation reduce Default Mode Network (DMN) activity, produce ego dissolution, and increase brain entropy — converging on the same neural state via different pathways.

## Counter-Arguments / 反驳与质疑

### 1. 结构类比不等于因果等价
The mapping between predictive coding and Yogacara is an *analogy*, not a proof of identity. Two frameworks can produce similar-looking diagrams while differing fundamentally in ontology, epistemology, and testable predictions. Structural isomorphism between models does not mean they describe the same reality.

### 2. 自由能原理的可证伪性问题
The Free Energy Principle has been criticized (e.g., by Beren Millidge, Matteo Colombo) as being so general that it risks unfalsifiability. If everything a living system does can be redescribed as "minimizing free energy," the principle may be more of a redescription than an explanation.

### 3. 预测编码非唯一范式
Predictive coding is influential but not the only computational neuroscience framework. Rival frameworks (e.g., Bayesian brain, neural Darwinism, global workspace theory) offer different accounts of perception. Presenting predictive coding as established fact overstates current consensus.

### 4. 选择性对应的风险
The video selects aspects of Yogacara that fit predictive coding while leaving aside elements that don't (e.g., the eight consciousnesses model's details about manas, the specific mechanics of karma and rebirth). This cherry-picking can make the correspondence look tighter than it is.

### 5. 冥想效果的因果归因问题
While neuroimaging shows DMN changes during meditation, correlation ≠ causation. The interpretation that meditation "lowers precision weights" is a theoretical gloss, not a directly measured variable. Precision weighting is a computational-level concept not yet directly observable in neural data.

### 6. "受控幻觉"隐喻的局限
Anil Seth's "controlled hallucination" framing is deliberately provocative. Critics (e.g., Andy Clark) note it can be misleading — perception is still constrained by and responsive to real-world structure. Calling it a "hallucination" may overstate the brain's independence from external reality.

### 7. 忽略了冥想传统的多样性
The video treats "meditation" as a monolithic practice, but Buddhist meditation traditions vary enormously (Theravada vs. Zen vs. Tibetan Dzogchen vs. Pure Land). The predictive coding mapping may fit Yogacara-influenced practices but not all contemplative traditions equally.

## Factual Claims Requiring Verification / 待验证事实

1. **"从高层脑区向低层感觉区域投射的反馈连接在数量上远远超过前馈连接"** — The ratio is well-supported in visual cortex literature (Felleman & Van Essen, 1991), but "far exceeds" is a simplification of a complex, region-dependent picture.

2. **"初级视觉皮层V1接收到的输入信号中，来自视网膜的信号只占很小一部分"** — This is a common claim in predictive coding literature but the exact proportions are debated. The claim is directionally correct but the emphasis on "very small" may overstate it.

3. **"深度冥想者的脑扫描显示默认模式网络活动大幅下降，全脑同步性上升"** — Supported by multiple studies (Brewer et al., 2011; Garrison et al., 2015) but effect sizes vary and "大幅" (dramatic) may overstate the magnitude for typical meditators vs. extreme long-term practitioners.

4. **"弗里斯顿大概不知道唯识宗"** — Unverified assumption. Friston has engaged with contemplative traditions in collaborative work (e.g., with monks at Mind & Life Institute). He may have some awareness of Yogacara.
