## Overview

Two distinct Bund trades triggered by fundamental catalysts: the **French election results** and the **ECB Press Conference**. Despite same instrument, they differed significantly in nature and management.

---

## Trade 1: ECB Press Conference (Quiet Session)

**Theme:** "Even during a relatively quiet ECB Press Conference, we look at how he was still able to scale up a position of over 200 lots with only 1 tick of risk."

### Correlation Factor
Negative correlation between **EURUSD and Bund futures** used as confidence signal. Small targets with dynamic sizing, max risk of **1-2 ticks offside.**

**Price ladder starts at:** 5:49

### Execution Sequence (Lot by Lot)

1. **Initial entry:** Started with **20 lots**, scaling passively as Euro hit lows

2. **Position building:**
   - Increased to **50 lots**
   - Advanced to **130 lots**
   - Built to **190 lots**
   - Used passive order placement (laying orders) for both entry and exit

3. **Scaling out:** Consistently **10 lots per price level** as trade progressed

4. **Reaction at highs:** Reduced from **190 lots back to 50 lots** -- core position only. Increased market reactivity at highs meant less conviction.

5. **Second wave (Euro revisits lows):**
   - Placed bids at levels 11 and 12
   - Received **80-lot fill at level 12**
   - Added **30 additional lots at level 13**
   - Partial fill served as confirmation signal for increased aggressiveness

   **Euro lows timestamp:** ~652 seconds into video

6. **Final adjustment:** With Euro still near lows and potential 5-10 additional ticks remaining, reduced per-level scale-out to **5 lots** (vs prior 10-lot increments) to maintain exposure through completion.

### Learning Questions & Answers
1. **Correlation leaned on:** EURUSD negative correlation with Bund
2. **Scaling-out technique:** 10 lots per price level (reduced to 5 near end)
3. **Reaction at highs:** Cut from 190 to 50 lots immediately

## Trade 2: French Election -- Eurex Open

**Theme:** "This trader executes immediately at the Eurex open in the German Bund... gets as aggressive as possible right at the open."

**Price ladder starts at:** 1-second mark (start of video)

### Execution Sequence (Lot by Lot)

1. **Initial entry:**
   - Filled **14 lots at 161.52**
   - Rapidly increased to **74 lots**
   - Average price: **161.45**
   - Exited majority quickly, retained **10 lots**
   - Immediately began adding again

2. **Second wave:**
   - Scaled back to **100 lots**
   - Observed **2,324 lots on the offer** (significant liquidity)
   - This large size = additional confidence signal
   - Market failed to supply additional offers → lifted aggressively through 2,324-lot level

3. **Critical moment -- offer pulled:**
   - When **2,329-lot offer disappeared** (was pulled), trader dramatically reduced to **24 lots**
   - Recognized the shifted market structure instantly

4. **Position management during retracement:**
   - Held average size while market compressed
   - Added back to **88 lots** as price resumed lower
   - Gradually wound down position
   - Maintained **20-30 lot average** until trade potential diminished

### Learning Questions
1. **When did trader initiate?** Immediately at the open (1 second)
2. **Scaling-out strategy?** Quick initial profit-take, then re-add on continuation
3. **Size management when offside?** Reduced quickly, never held full size offside
4. **Reaction when 2,329 lot pulled?** Immediately cut to 24 lots
