## Core Teaching

How to interpret order flow at critical market junctures using Bund and Eurostoxx as correlated pairs. Focus on significant price levels rather than every tick.

### What Is a "Key Point"?

> "It's the point in the market where based on previous insights you are expecting something to happen."

Some traders fixate on every uptick and downtick -- useful for short-term ladder scalps, but the essential skill is matching ladder activity to specific expectations.

---

## The Fundamental Rule

> "Order flow should fit that strategy. This could be your final check in the box of conviction on a trade."

Without matching price action to expectations, confidence should diminish. Larger positions require recognizing conditions that align with prior observations.

---

## SIZE = SETUP + ORDER FLOW

> "Knowing when to trade big size in the market is one of the most important differentiating skills of Elite Traders."

Position size = function of setup + order flow alignment. Greater alignment → larger positions. Setup without matching order flow → tradeable but requires cautious sizing.

---

## The Setup: Monday, June 1, 2020

### Bund Analysis (Market Profile)
- **Strong opening drive downward** breaking previous day's value area
- Price trades back to value area bottom (not higher), then approaches cash open
- Breakdown occurs at cash open
- **Pickup in pace** signals favorable price action

### Eurostoxx Analysis (Market Profile)
- Opens **above previous day value area** with strong initial balance upward
- At cash open, price sits near **Initial Balance top**
- These prices represent the **highest levels since early March** (COVID context)
- Broke the **3096 level** -- a price point participants were monitoring

### Critical Observation

> "The technical landscape is very bullish, at this key point we should see strong buying as we are moving to the open space toward the upside. However, the buyers' strength is not showing up, and this is very important information."

---

## Price Ladder Analysis -- 1 Minute Into Cash Session

### Key Levels
- Eurostoxx Initial Balance high: **3106**
- Bund Initial Balance low: **172.04**

### What Happened at 172.04

- **400+ lots** trade into the bid
- Immediately shift to offer side
- Next price trades with **minimal buying support on the offer**
- Creates a **downward snap** through IB low
- Described as: "fast snap, almost no pullback, and clear initiative change"

**Interpretation:** As long as markets maintain below .04, the move retains downside potential.

---

## The Correlation Failure

At this juncture, Eurostoxx should rally (bullish technical setup + Bund selling). But:

- Approaching **3105** level, which holds **600+ lots** (not a lot for this market)
- Buyers cannot overcome selling resistance
- Trading into this level causes offers to reappear and pressure price down

### Critical Warning Signal

> Each time Bund trades lower prices, offer orders reappear on Eurostoxx ladders.

When Stoxx backs off quickly with no Bund buying initiative, seller initiative remains intact. Low volume = buyer withdrawal.

**Conclusion:** Short Bund positions remain comfortable. Executing long Eurostoxx becomes inadvisable because order flow contradicts expectations and demonstrates buyer weakness.

---

## The Result

While Bund sold off post-IB breakdown, Eurostoxx not only failed breaking its IB high but declined to **3087** -- significant equity weakness.

---

## Secondary Trade Setup

Bund weakness may have prevented Eurostoxx rollover. If Bund shows buying initiative → Eurostoxx acceleration likely downward.

### Pivot Point: 171.80 Area

- Stabilization zone for next phase
- Time spent and volume accumulated reveal buyer presence

### Structural Detail

- Clear low volume at **171.84**
- Breaking through this price = potential seller capitulation
- New sellers entering Bund at these levels are most responsive to reversals

### Order Flow Pace

Trading through **171.84** shows "very similar" pace to the earlier 172.04 breakdown -- "fast snap, minimal pullback, clear initiative change."

### Trapped Seller Analysis

Traders selling below .84 are now positioned to lose. Creates leverage for **dual positions: buying Bund while selling Stoxx** (which previously showed weakness).

### Exit Conditions

Staying above **171.84** in Bund and below **3087** in Eurostoxx allows confidence in letting markets develop.

---

## Key Principles

1. **Order flow must match the trade type** -- if it doesn't, reduce size or skip
2. **Correlation failure is information** -- when a correlated market should move but doesn't, that's a powerful signal
3. **SIZE = SETUP + ORDER FLOW** -- position size is earned by alignment
4. **Pattern development:** Consistently compare expectation vs. reality to build personal pattern inventory
5. **Ask:** "What did I think would happen? What 'should' have happened? What actually transpired? What does this reveal?"
