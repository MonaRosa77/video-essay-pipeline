# Price Ladder (DOM) — Learning Resources

**What it is**: The Depth of Market / order book display showing live bids and offers at each price level. The core tool for all Axia Futures scalping strategies — used to read absorption, failed follow-through, personality shifts, and trapped participants in real time.

**Used in**: All 6 scalping strategies (Exhaustion Flick, Exhaustion Absorption, Trapped Participants Reversal, Trend Scalp, Continuation Scalp, Failed Breakout Reversal)
