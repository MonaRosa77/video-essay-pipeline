# Market Profile — Learning Resources

**What it is**: A TPO (Time Price Opportunity) chart showing how much time price spent at each level during a session. Reveals Initial Balance (first hour's range), Value Area (where 70% of time was spent), opening drives, single prints, poor highs/lows, and day type classification.

**Used in**: Trend Scalp (identifying trending days, initial balance breaks), Failed Breakout Reversal (range context), Continuation Scalp (identifying directional days after data releases)
