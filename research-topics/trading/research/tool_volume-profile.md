# Volume Profile — Learning Resources

**What it is**: A histogram showing volume traded at each price level over a period. Reveals High Volume Nodes (HVN = areas of acceptance/traps), Low Volume Nodes (LVN = areas of rejection/fast moves), Value Area (70% of volume), and Point of Control (POC = most traded price).

**Used in**: Exhaustion Flick (one-time-framing structure), Trapped Participants Reversal (HVN/LVN identification), Trend Scalp (untested levels, gaps), Failed Breakout Reversal (range context)
