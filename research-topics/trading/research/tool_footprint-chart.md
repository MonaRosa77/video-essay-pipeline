# Footprint Chart — Learning Resources

**What it is**: A chart type that shows volume traded at the bid vs. ask at each price level within each candle. Reveals absorption (passive buyer/seller absorbing aggression), delta shifts (who's in control), stacked imbalances (institutional activity), and aggression patterns invisible on regular charts.

**Used in**: Exhaustion Absorption (confirming passive seller absorbing), Trapped Participants Reversal (confirming delta shift at extremes), Failed Breakout Reversal (seeing aggression fail inside the candle)
